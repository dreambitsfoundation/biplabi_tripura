About Us

www.indianconsumers.org is designed and maintained by the Consumers’ Protection Association (CPA India). The interactive website www.indianconsumers.org was launched in 2009. But the website was hacked by the enemies of Indian Consumers. Now it has been redesigned and launched again to publish consumer complaints and company responses on settlement of complaints. The website shall publish important news, views, judgments and law concerning consumers. All are requested to involve in this dream project and make it a success. CPA India was established in 1984 and registered in 1987 under the Societies Registration Act 1860. CPA INDIA was invited in 1985 by the Govt of India to participate in the discussions before finalizing the Consumer Protection Bill 1986. Formerly member of CCPC, BIS, CPA India was also a member of CAG registered with TRAI and an Investors Association recognized by SEBI. CPA INDIA works in close co-operation with civil society and Government and received National Award for works in the interest of consumers. Dignified Guests to CPA India's past programmes are: Central Ministers, Chief Ministers, Vice-Chancellor, Law makers, representatives from MRTP Commission, National Consumer Court (NCDRC), Bureau of India Standards, Consumer Organizations & Activists from many states, Friedrich Naumann Stiftung (Germany). Chairman of TRAI, Chairman of SEBI. CPA India has organized National Seminar on Quality of telephone services and role of TRAI, Two-day National Consumer Convention on Consumer Justice- 2007 in New Delhi. As part of celebration of 25 years of voluntary consumer service, it organized National Seminar on Telecom Services and National Seminar of Investors Services. Chairman of TRAI and SEBI participated. President of this organization was elected Chairman of the CCC India, apex consumer body of leading consumer organizations in India. He was member of the Govt of India Task Force on UN Revisesd Guidelines on Consumer Protection. We intend to provide legal information to all consumers via website. We request you to support us to maintain this website & Consumer Education.
